Fitbit Coach App

This is a small personal application used to connect Fitbit data for personal health and fitness tracking. It is designed to access authorized Fitbit data such as activity, heart rate, sleep, profile, and weight data for personal use.

The app is not a medical service and does not provide diagnosis or treatment. It exists so the paperwork has a home and the software can behave like a grown-up.
