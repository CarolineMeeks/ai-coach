Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

This application connects to Fitbit only to access the data needed to support personal health and fitness tracking.

What data is used

The application may access Fitbit data such as activity, heart rate, sleep, profile, and weight information that you authorize through Fitbit.

How data is used

Your data is used only to display, review, and analyze your own personal health and fitness information.

Sharing

Your Fitbit data is not sold and is not shared with third parties except as required by law.

Storage

Authorized tokens and downloaded data may be stored locally for personal use.

Your control

You can revoke access at any time through your Fitbit account settings.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact Caroline Meeks through GitHub at github.com/CarolineMeeks.
